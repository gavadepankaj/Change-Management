Customer Preference Tracking

Tracks each customer's dairy product preferences. This information can be used to personalize product recommendations and marketing campaigns, enhancing customer satisfaction.

Automated Milk Delivery Scheduling

Allows customers to schedule their milk deliveries based on their preferred frequency. This not only ensures timely deliveries but also lets customers have control over their orders.

Subscription Management

Enables customers to manage their dairy product subscriptions easily. Customers can modify their subscription preferences, pause deliveries, or update billing information within their accounts.

After customers receive their dairy products, they can provide feedback directly through the website, allowing you to address concerns and improve the overall quality.

Interactive Customer Surveys

Regularly conduct interactive surveys through the CRM to gather insights into customer preferences, needs, and opinions. This data can guide product development and marketing strategies.

Customer Support Chatbot

Assists customers with common inquiries, order tracking, and product information. This can enhance customer support efficiency and availability.
